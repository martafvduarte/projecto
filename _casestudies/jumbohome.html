The Problem

Jumbo product directory is all mixed up and home department products were spread between a lot of categories on the website. There was a printed home catalog coming out every year and people wouldn’t be able to find this home collections on the website, which meant a very small amount of sales of this kind of products trought the website.

There were a lot to technical restrictions since the website is build using an intricate and old technology.

Goal

The goal was to make home products easier to locate on the website and increase the sales of this kid of products throught this channel. It was expected to work as a digital and functional compliment of the printed home catalog.

Key Finds

  • Most Home decor sellers take an inspirational aproach (inspirational images and decor tips);
  • Products categorized by room;
  • Mainly two ways to search: trend and room;

Solution

We created a separate area for all the home products and collections on the website. This new area looked like an independent space within the website where we could easily browse trough an index of all the home sub-categories of products and also view the seasonal campaigns in an inspirational way, taking ideas, interacting with the images and easily navigating to the product page where it would be possible to add it to the shopping cart.
